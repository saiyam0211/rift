Day 1 : The Rift Opens

09:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Arrival & Registration

Gate Opens. Teams arrive at the venue.
Check-in: QR Code scan, ID verification.
Kit Distribution: ID Cards, Swag Bags, Wi-Fi Credentials.
Seat Allocation: Teams settle down at their designated tables.

11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Opening Ceremony & Speaker Sessions

Welcome Note: Introduction to RIFT & PW IOI.
Speaker Sessions: Industry Leaders & Guest Speakers share insights on Open Innovation.
Rules Briefing: Submission guidelines and judging criteria.

12:30 PM

The Reveal

Problem Statements Unlocked: The 4 Open Innovation challenges are revealed simultaneously across all 4 cities.
HACKING BEGINS: Zero Hour. Brainstorming starts immediately.

12:30 PM - 01:30 PM

Ideation & Brainstorming

Teams discuss the Problem Statements and decide their approach.

01:00 PM - 03:30 PM

Lunch Break

04:00 PM - 08:00 PM

Mentoring Window Open

Idea Validation: You can visit mentoring window to check the approach, tech stack, and feasibility. Guidance on pivoting if necessary.

06:00 PM - 08:00 PM

Strategy Lock (Form Filling)

Mandatory Step: Teams must fill the official form to lock their Team Name and Selected Problem Statement (PS). No changes allowed after this.

08:00 PM - 10:30 PM

Dinner

10:00 PM - 11:59 PM

Mini-Event 1

Gaming/Fun Activity: A quick 30-min energizer (e.g., Typing speed test, CS Quiz, or rapid-fire gaming) to break the monotony.

11:59 PM

Midnight Checkpoint

Halfway Mark: 12 Hours down. Teams should ideally have their backend/core logic ready.

Midnight Snacks

07:00 AM - 09:30 AM

Breakfast

Code Freeze Warning

HACKING ENDS

01:00 PM - 05:00 PM

Judging (Expo Mode)

The Pitch: Judges visit tables. Teams demonstrate live working prototypes. Top teams shortlisted for stage pitch (optional depending on volume).

05:00 PM - 06:00 PM

Closing Ceremony

Results: Winner announcements.
Photo Session: Group photo.
Vote of Thanks.

06:00 PM

Dispersal
